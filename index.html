THE SECRET VAULT

We dance to express, not impress

Welcome to The Secret Vault, London's dance hub! Elevate your skills with our expert instructors and master impactful moves in a dynamic, diverse setting. Whether you're a novice or seasoned dancer, join us for a transformative experience where every beat is an opportunity for growth.

  • From beginner, to intermediate and advances, we have dance classes which accommodate all levels.
  • We have public dance classes where you can learn and perform together or we can also host private one to one or group sessions.
