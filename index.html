THE SECRET VAULT

We dance to express, not impress

Welcome to The Secret Vault, London's dance company! Elevate your skills with our expert instructors and master impactful moves in a dynamic, diverse setting. Whether you're a novice or seasoned dancer, join us for a transformative experience where every beat is an opportunity for growth.

CLASSES

4-5 Classes a month

40 per class (max)

Expertly Recorded

  • From beginner and intermediate to professional and advanced, we have dance classes which accommodate all levels.
  • We have public dance classes where you can learn and perform together or we can also host private one to one or group sessions.
  • Every single public dance class gets professionally recorded and uploaded to our YouTube channel. If you have an idea for a choreographed dance we can facilitate sessions to bring your vision to life.
